Royale Palace

This unassuming hotel in a remote mountain village is 3 km from the shores of Nakki Lake, 4 km from the
11th-century Dilwara Temples and 24 km from the NH27.
Simple rooms feature flat-screen TVs, minibars, and tea and coffeemaking facilities. Family rooms with
sofabeds sleep up to 4 people. Room service is available 24/7.
