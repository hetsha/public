Conrad Dubai

on Sheikh Zayed Road, the property is 2-minute walk from Dubai World Trade Centre and is close to Dubai
International Financial Centre and The Dubai Mall. The hotel was officially opened on 18 September 2013.
Conrad Dubai hotel has three food and beverage venues: Ballaro, Bliss 6, Cave, Isla and Kimpo.
