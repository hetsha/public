The Lake View Hotel

Set on a narrow street, this down-to-earth hotel with views of Lake Pichola is a 14-minute walk from the
16th-century City Palace and 3 km from Udaipur City train station.
The 10 unpretentious rooms with warm decor provide free Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs and fans; some add sitting
areas. Room service is available.
