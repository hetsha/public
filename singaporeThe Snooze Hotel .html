The Snooze Hotel

About this hotel
Set within a Colonial-style building in a row of shops, this unpretentious budget hotel is 2 km from both
East Coast Beach and a metro station. Gardens by the Bay nature park is 10 km away.
Informal, streamlined rooms feature skylights, and offer Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs and en suite bathrooms.
Upgraded rooms have floor-to-ceiling windows. Room service is available.
