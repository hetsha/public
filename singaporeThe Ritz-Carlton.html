The Ritz-Carlton

Overlooking Marina Bay, this luxury hotel is a 7-minute walk from the iconic Singapore Flyer.
Featuring city or bay views, the polished rooms offer free Wi-Fi and flat-screens, as well as marble
bathrooms with rainfall showers and separate soaking tubs. Upgraded rooms have access to a club lounge. Luxe
suites add living rooms. Room service is available 24/7.
Dining options include 2 restaurants, a bakery, and a poolside bar, plus a swanky lounge with live music and
glass sculptures. There's also an outdoor pool, a chic spa and a kids' club.
