Grand Copthorne Waterfront

Overlooking the Singapore River, this upscale, high-rise hotel is 7 km from the beaches on
Sentosa island and 6 km from the Universal Studios Singapore amusement park.
Streamlined rooms feature minibars, free Wi-Fi and flat-screen TVs; upgraded rooms feature marble bathrooms
and/or river views.; luxe 1- and 2-bedroom apartments include full kitchens. Room service is available.
Breakfast, parking and a local shuttle are free. There's a 1940's Shanghai-style restaurant, a casual
international eatery and an Italian grill, plus 2 bars. Other amenities include a fitness centre, a pool & a
hot tub, along with a salon.
